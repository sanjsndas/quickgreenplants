🌿 Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How quickly do you respond to gardening questions?

Our horticulture team aims to reply within 24-48 hours. For urgent plant issues, check our detailed "Plant Doctor" section (part of the 78k+ word library). You can also reach us via Instagram DM for faster tips.

🌱 Do you offer personalized garden plans?

Yes! We provide custom garden design consultations (virtual or in-person in Portland area). Fill the contact form with “Garden Design Inquiry” and we’ll schedule a free 15-min discovery call.

📦 Do you sell seeds or plants?

We focus on education and resources. However, we recommend local organic seed libraries and ethical nurseries. In our “Seed & Tool Guide” (available on the homepage), we list trusted partners.

🤝 How can I collaborate or write for Quick Green Plants?

We love contributions from experienced gardeners, botanists, and permaculture designers. Send us a pitch via the contact form with “Guest Article” and a short writing sample. We’ll be in touch!
